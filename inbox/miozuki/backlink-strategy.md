## Overview

A new domain needs 25-50 dofollow links from 25+ unique referring domains before Google ranks non-brand keywords with any confidence. Quality outweighs quantity: one link from Stuff.co.nz or Viva is worth more than fifty low-DA directory links. The strategy runs on two parallel tracks:

- **Content track:** publish linkable assets (guides, comparisons, resource pages) that earn links passively and rank independently
- **Outreach track:** actively place links via directories, editorial media, blogs, and influencers

Both tracks are required. Content without outreach stays invisible on a new domain. Outreach without strong content produces low-DA links that don't move rankings.

**Target:** 18-33 unique referring domains in 90 days. 50 unique referring domains in 6 months. Both are achievable without a paid link-building budget.

## Months 1-3: Foundation

### Content to publish

1. Audit and optimise the existing "Moissanite vs Diamond NZ" article for featured snippet (answer in first 100 words, comparison table, FAQ schema)
2. Moissanite grades and brands guide NZ (Forever One vs unbranded vs Harro Gem -- what to look for)
3. Pearl jewellery guide NZ (types, quality grading, care, where to buy NZ)
4. Collection page copy: add 200-300 words of descriptive copy above the product grid on each major collection page

Once published, pitch each guide to SourceBottle contacts and wedding bloggers as a reference resource.

### Outreach to execute

**Directories (week 1-2, low effort, immediate):**
- Submit to Finda.co.nz, Yellow Pages NZ, NZ Business Directory, Hotfrog NZ
- Register NZBN if not already done; confirm Miozuki appears in NZBN search
- Create and verify Google Business Profile
- Submit to JWNZ directory (jwnz.co.nz) -- low DA (~21) but industry-relevant

**SourceBottle (week 2, ongoing):**
- Register at sourcebottle.com (free)
- Set alerts for: jewellery, engagement, wedding, ethical shopping, sustainable fashion, consumer trends NZ
- Respond to relevant NZ journalist queries as brand founder
- One Stuff or Viva placement from SourceBottle equals 20 directory backlinks in SEO authority

**First editorial pitch (month 2):**
- Ensemble Magazine has closed (ceased publishing 2025). Remove from outreach plan.
- Alternative targets: Fashion Quarterly (fqmagazine.co.nz) or Next Magazine for NZ fashion/lifestyle coverage
- Pitch Bride and Groom Magazine (brideandgroom.co.nz, production@brideandgroom.co.nz) in month 2 for engagement ring editorial coverage

## Months 4-6: Topical Depth

### Content to publish

5. Lab diamond vs moissanite NZ comparison (target lab diamond cluster with moissanite-favourable framing)
6. Moissanite shape guide NZ (round vs oval vs cushion vs pear -- which suits which hand shape)
7. Ethical engagement ring guide NZ (positions moissanite and pearl as the answer)
8. Affordable engagement rings NZ under $500 (transactional, direct conversion target)
9. **Begin NZ Moissanite Guide** as a content hub on the existing Next.js stack (see directory-seo-feasibility.md). Target: 2-3 guide pages per month from this point. Host at `/moissanite-guide` on miozuki.co.nz (subdirectory -- not a subdomain; subdirectory consolidates domain authority).

### Outreach to execute

**Wedding and lifestyle media:**
- Pitch Bride and Groom Magazine (brideandgroom.co.nz) for engagement ring feature -- standard editorial section, appropriate for a moissanite brand. DA moderate, highly relevant audience.
- Pitch Viva (NZ Herald): "NZ-designed moissanite jewellery as ethical alternative to mined diamonds" or "pearl revival as 2026 jewellery trend." Lead time 4-8 weeks. Contact: viva@nzherald.co.nz *(verify before sending).*
- Pitch Stuff Life and Style via SourceBottle or direct editorial contact -- more accessible than NZME for new brands.

**Wedding and lifestyle blogs:**
- Hollow and Co (hollowauco.com): NZ wedding blog. Pitch a ring reveal or styled shoot feature.
- NZ wedding photographers with editorial blogs: a styled shoot collaboration produces product photography for Miozuki AND an editorial post with a backlink from the photographer's site. Target 2-3 photographers.
- Feedspot NZ jewellery blogs (35 indexed): most DA 21-29 but topically relevant. Guest posts or product review placements achievable.

**Styled shoot strategy:**
A styled shoot (product + NZ photographer + NZ model) produces: product photography, editorial blog post from photographer, potential placement in Bride and Groom or Ensemble. Three outputs from one shoot. Invest early.

- Pitch the moissanite care and resale value guides to jewellery educators and consumer advice bloggers as reference links.
- Monitor for NZ press opportunities via SourceBottle on an ongoing basis.

## Outreach Channels

### Directories (immediate)
- Finda.co.nz
- Yellow Pages NZ (yellow.co.nz)
- NZ Business Directory (nzbizdir.co.nz)
- Hotfrog NZ (hotfrog.co.nz)
- JWNZ (jwnz.co.nz/directory) -- trade directory, low DA but topical
- Google Business Profile -- not a backlink but essential for local trust

### Editorial media (months 2-6)
| Publication | Audience | Contact | Pitch angle |
|---|---|---|---|
| Fashion Quarterly (fq.co.nz) | NZ fashion + lifestyle | fq.co.nz/fq-living-project-submission/ | New NZ brand story, moissanite + pearl, ethical sourcing. (Ensemble Magazine closed 2025.) |
| Bride and Groom Magazine (brideandgroom.co.nz) | NZ engaged couples | production@brideandgroom.co.nz | Engagement ring feature, moissanite alternative |
| Viva (NZ Herald) | 259k weekly, NZ women | viva@nzherald.co.nz *(verify)* | Ethical diamond alternative, pearl revival |
| Stuff Life and Style | Broad NZ readership | Via SourceBottle or direct | Consumer trend angles, ethical jewellery |
| Metro NZ | Auckland, quarterly | Via site contact | Premium Auckland brand story |

### Journalist sourcing
- **SourceBottle (sourcebottle.com):** free, NZ-specific HARO equivalent. Best channel for editorial backlinks at zero cost. Set alerts and respond consistently.
- **Featured.com (formerly HARO):** quality has declined due to AI flooding. Use as secondary to SourceBottle.

### Wedding and lifestyle blogs (months 3-9)
- Hollow and Co (hollowauco.com): NZ wedding editorial blog
- NZ wedding photographers with blogs: styled shoot collaboration (2-3 photographers)
- Feedspot NZ jewellery blogs: 35 indexed, DA 21-29, guest posts achievable
- NZ ethical/sustainable shopping blogs: "alternatives to mined diamonds NZ" roundup placement

### Community and social (indirect, ongoing)
Reddit, Pinterest, and Instagram do not pass strong link equity directly. They produce referral traffic and brand search volume, which Google treats as authority signals for new domains. See community-seo-strategy.md for the full Reddit and Pinterest strategy. These are indirect signals, not substitutes for editorial backlinks.
