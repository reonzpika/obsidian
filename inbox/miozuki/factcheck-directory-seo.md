## Claim 8: Subdomain vs subdirectory -- authority transfer "partial and contested"

**Result: VERIFIED**

Current 2026 SEO consensus (Semrush, Koanthic, Embarque, Design Spartans) confirms: subdirectories consolidate domain authority more effectively than subdomains for content hubs. Google's John Mueller states both are treated equally in theory, but real-world performance consistently favours subdirectories. Multiple 2026 guides explicitly recommend subdirectories as the default for content hubs unless there is a specific reason for separation. The doc's characterisation is accurate and current.

**Implication for Miozuki:** Build the NZ Moissanite Guide as `/guide` or `/moissanite-guide` on miozuki.co.nz, not as a subdomain. This is the stronger SEO choice.
