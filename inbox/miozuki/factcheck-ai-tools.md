## Claim 8: Klaviyo (free tier 250 profiles / 500 emails; 500 contacts ~USD $20/month)

**Status: VERIFIED with important caveat**

Free tier: 250 active profiles, 500 email sends/month -- confirmed. 500 contacts: USD $20/month email plan (251-500 active profiles, 5,000 sends/month) -- confirmed.

**Critical caveat not in doc:** When Klaviyo syncs with Shopify, it pulls ALL customers (including historical purchasers) as active profiles -- not just email subscribers. A store with 300 past buyers will exceed the 250-profile free limit immediately, before a single campaign is sent. For Miozuki at launch this is low risk (pre-revenue, no existing customer base), but the first real purchase wave will push the list into paid territory faster than expected.

**Action required:** Add caveat: "Note: Shopify sync counts all historical customers as active profiles. The 250-profile free tier is consumed by existing customers, not just new subscribers."
