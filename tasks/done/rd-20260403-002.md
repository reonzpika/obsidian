Define the Care Gap Finder task in precise, testable terms. Step 1 deliverable for Care Gap Finder — needed before any architecture selection, data modelling, or prototype work begins.

## What to define

**Care gaps in scope (MVP)**
- List each care gap by name and clinical condition (e.g. HbA1c monitoring for Type 2 diabetes, CVDRA for patients 45+ with cardiovascular risk factors, BP measurement for hypertension, annual medication reviews for polypharmacy)
- Mark which are in scope for Objective 1 (synthetic data testing) vs deferred to Objective 2 (real data)

**Clinical rules (one per care gap)**
- Write the formal rule: patient criteria + data condition + time threshold
- Example: "Patient with ICD-10 E11.x AND no HbA1c result recorded in the last 12 months = care gap"
- For each rule: what diagnosis codes qualify, what result type is checked, what date field is used, what is the lookback window

**Data requirements**
- For each rule, list the exact Medtech/Indici fields required (diagnosis fields, result type codes, dates, demographics)
- Flag any data that may not be available in the sandbox environment

**Success criteria — operationalise the target**
- Choose the metric: precision, recall, F1, or something else? Justify clinically — missing a care gap vs false-flagging a non-gap are not equivalent.
- Define the test set: how many patients, what mix of care gap / no care gap per condition?
- Define edge cases and how they must be labelled: new patient with no history, patient who formally declined screening, result received outside PMS

**Deliverable:** A written specification document. MBIE evidence for Step 1 of Objective 1.
