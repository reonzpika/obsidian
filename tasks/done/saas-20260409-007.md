## Context

The homepage (`app/page.tsx`) currently has one image: the portrait in the hero (DrRyoEguchiProfilePicMain.jpg, being separately regenerated in saas-20260409-008). The § 02 "The story" section is a pure-text blockquote with an author chip — it carries significant emotional weight but no supporting image.

The roadmap section (§ 04) and principles section (§ 03) are card-based and don't need imagery. The best candidate is a single editorial mid-page image placed in or adjacent to the story section — something that shows Ryo's actual clinical context without restaging the hero portrait.

## Image brief

A candid documentary shot of a GP's consultation desk — no person required, or person partially in frame. Conveys the clinical reality that the story section describes: the pile of admin, the late notes, the laptop open at end of day.

Two options to generate and evaluate:

### Option A — End of day, empty room
Consultation room late afternoon. Empty chair opposite (patient gone). Desk with laptop open showing a clinical notes interface, a cold mug of tea, a stack of patient folders, a stethoscope draped over one corner. Venetian blinds casting long late-afternoon light stripes. No person. The image reads "the work that outlasts the patients."

### Option B — GP at laptop, candid
GP from behind or three-quarter rear — face not visible or barely visible in profile — typing at a laptop in a consultation room. Relaxed posture, end-of-session feel. Window light from the left. The mood is: "this is the daily reality the story is about."

Option A is safer (no person = no anatomy issues). Option B is more human.

## Technical brief (both options)

- Camera: Fujifilm X-T5 or Leica M11, 35mm equiv, f/2.0–2.8
- Time of day: late afternoon, warm 4500K
- Colour grade: Classic Chrome or Kodak Portra 400 — muted greens, warm midtones
- Aspect: 16:9 for widescreen placement, or 4:3 if used as a medium-width inset
- Size: 2K
- Model: `gemini-3-pro-image-preview`
