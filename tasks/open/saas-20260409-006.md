## Context

The referral-images landing page (`app/(marketing)/referral-images/page.tsx`) currently has one image: the hero (`referral_images_hero_image_3.png` — GP holding phone, desktop monitor in background). Six sections have no imagery at all. Two sections are strong candidates.

## Target sections

### § 01 — The problem (pull-quotes + three pain-point cards)

No image. This section articulates the "emailing photos to yourself / WhatsApp" problem. A good image here would show the messy status-quo: a GP at a desk looking at their personal phone camera roll trying to find a referral photo, or a desktop screen with a long email chain of photo attachments. Conveys the friction before ClinicPro.

**Shot brief:**
- GP at desktop, phone in hand, looking slightly frustrated or focused
- Phone screen shows a camera roll — lots of clinical photos mixed with personal photos (blurred for privacy)
- Desktop shows an email composition window with attachments
- Documentary style — not staged, not stock
- Lighting: window light from one side, afternoon, venetian blinds optional
- Camera: Fujifilm X-T4 or similar, 35mm, f/2.2, natural grain
- Colour: desaturated, muted, Classic Chrome

### § 02 — How it works (numbered steps 1–4)

No image. This section describes the workflow. A good image here would show the moment of capture: GP's phone camera pointed at a clinical finding on a patient's arm or back (patient off-frame), desktop monitor visible in the soft background already showing the ClinicPro interface. The contrast between the phone (foreground, capturing) and the desktop (background, ready) makes the product's value legible in one frame.

**Shot brief:**
- Close-up/medium of hands holding phone in portrait orientation, camera app open
- Patient's forearm or back visible at edge of frame (blurred, no identifying features)
- Desktop in bokeh background — recognisable as a monitor with a light interface
- GP white-coat-free — wearing plain professional clothing
- Shallow depth of field — phone sharp, background bokeh
- Camera: Fujifilm X100V, 35mm equiv, f/2.0, ISO 800
- Colour: warm, natural skin tones, no over-saturation

## Generation workflow

Use `scripts/gen-image.mjs` with `--model gemini-3-pro-image-preview`.

Prompt files: create at `scripts/prompts/referral-images-problem.prompt.md` and `scripts/prompts/referral-images-workflow.prompt.md`.

Output paths:
- `public/generated/referral-images-problem.jpg`
- `public/generated/referral-images-workflow.jpg`

Run command example:
```
node --env-file=.env scripts/gen-image.mjs \
  --prompt scripts/prompts/referral-images-problem.prompt.md \
  --out public/generated/referral-images-problem.jpg \
  --model gemini-3-pro-image-preview \
  --aspect 16:9 --size 2K
```

Wire into page with hairline frame + CornerBrackets pattern (same as AI Scribe page screenshots). Do not add captions.
