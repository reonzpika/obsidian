## Key themes from Nick Buist (2026-04-21)

Full notes in `nexwave-rd/docs/obj-1/output/gp-review-nick-2026-04-21.md`.

**Inbox Helper:**
- 4 urgency labels may be overcomplicating — possibly simplify to 2 (action within 24h vs. routine)
- Letter summarisation less valuable than assumed; GPs don't find it a big time sink
- Medical reconciliation from discharge summaries is the higher-value extraction task
- Auto-classification/READ coding on letters is valuable
- Test with SMS/text channel for truly urgent results

**Care Gap Finder:**
- Remove BP or deprioritise — low practice interest at scale
- Funding linkage is critical to adoption: target only PHO-funded gaps (diabetes, asthma, immunisation, screening). Research what attracts PHO/capitation funding in NZ before finalising scope.

**Other:**
- Nick mentioned Epic (https://epic.akohiringa.co.nz/) and TestSafe as worth understanding. Investigate separately when time allows.
