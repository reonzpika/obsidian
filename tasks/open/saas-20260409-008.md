## Problem

Current profile photo (`public/images/landing-page/DrRyoEguchiProfilePicMain.jpg`) reads as AI-generated. Common tells: plastic skin smoothing, over-even lighting, symmetrical composition, too-perfect features. A GP founder using this as a trust signal on a clinical product page is undermined if it looks synthetic.

The photo is used in:
- Homepage hero (right column, `aspect-[4/5]`, `object-top`)
- Homepage § 02 story section (48px circle author chip)
- AI Scribe page § 05 "Why I built this" (48px circle)
- Referral Images page (48px circle, same pattern)

The replacement must work at both scales: large portrait (homepage hero) and small circle crop (all other uses).

## What to fix in the prompt

The current image likely failed on these axes — address each explicitly:

1. **Skin texture:** Add "visible pores, natural skin texture, slight asymmetry in facial features, faint shadow under jaw and nasolabial fold" — not "smooth skin" or "flawless complexion"
2. **Lighting:** Use single directional light source only — "window light from camera-left, no fill light, no rim light, no studio softbox" — allows natural shadow falloff which reads as real
3. **Composition:** Off-centre subject, rule of thirds — never centred — "face occupies right two-thirds of frame, empty space camera-left"
4. **Pose:** Semi-candid, slight downward gaze or gaze just off-lens — "not looking directly at camera, turning slightly toward the window, relaxed jaw, natural resting expression" — not posed smile
5. **Camera vocabulary:** Specify a real camera body and lens — "shot on Leica M11 with 50mm Summicron at f/2.0, ISO 800, 1/125" — the model interprets this as a real photographic situation
6. **Film emulation:** "Kodak Tri-X 400 pushed one stop" (black and white) or "Kodak Portra 400, muted midtones" (colour) — film grain is the single biggest anti-AI signal
7. **Background:** Simple, slightly out of focus — a consultation room wall or a window — not a neutral studio backdrop
8. **Clothing:** Plain professional but not formal — open collar, no tie — GP-appropriate

## Recommended approach

Start with black and white (Tri-X 400 emulation) — grain and tonal range mask more AI artefacts than colour. Generate 4–6 variations, discard any with plastic skin or symmetrical composition immediately. The best result at large size will also work best as a small circle.
