Cultural competency is assessed across ALL five scoring domains in every consultation — it is not a separate criterion. The Hui Process and Meihana Model must be integrated naturally.

Score 3 (pass threshold) requires: "demonstrates culturally safe consultation and attempts to integrate aspects of a Māori model of health."
Score 5 requires: "Hui Process incorporated naturally into the consultation."

Resources:
- Meihana Model: https://www.rnzcgp.org.nz/news/equity/the-meihana-model/
- Hauora Māori Reflection template (obsidian/temp/)
- Hui Process — whakawhānaungatanga at opening of every consultation

Practice applying it in every consultation, not just Māori patients.
