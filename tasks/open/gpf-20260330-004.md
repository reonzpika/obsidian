Randomly select 10 patient records (consecutive appointments from a random start time on a random day). All records must have an entry in the past 12 months.

Complete the Module 2 recording sheet for each record, assessing criteria 1–17 (contemporaneous notes, background history, management plans, medications, referrals, screening, immunisation, risk factors, etc.).

Then complete the Report and Plan template identifying areas for development and improvement plan.

Return both the Recording Sheet and Report and Plan to GPEP Programme Advisor.

Note: Valid record review = completed within 6 months of the Fellowship Assessment Visit date.
