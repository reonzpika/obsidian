Implement the trust signals identified as requiring no additional research — these can ship immediately and should precede any ad spend optimisation.

## Scope

1. **Returns policy on PDPs** — visible returns statement on every product page, not just footer. "Free returns within 30 days for unworn items." Link to full policy page.
2. **NZBN in footer** — display NZ Business Number. Signals registered NZ business to cautious buyers.
3. **NZ address and contact** — physical NZ address and NZ phone number visible on footer and Contact page.
4. **Afterpay badge** — Afterpay logo and "or 4 payments of $X with Afterpay" on PDPs and cart. Absence is a trust negative in ANZ.
5. **Craftsmanship warranty statement** — minimum 1-year warranty on all pieces. Display on PDP and a dedicated Warranty page.
6. **SSL / payment trust badges at checkout** — Visa, Mastercard, Afterpay logos visible in cart and checkout footer.

## Notes

- Priority: returns policy and Afterpay badge have highest individual conversion impact
- NZBN display: check current NZ business registration status before adding — must be accurate
- Do not add empty review widget placeholders — zero-star displays increase abandonment vs. no widget

Depends on: none (implement independently of research tracks)
