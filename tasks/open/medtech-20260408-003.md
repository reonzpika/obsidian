Email sent to Defne (alexsupport@medtechglobal.com) on 8 April requesting:
1. Confirmation whether patient-based Invoice search (`Invoice?subject=Patient/{id}`) is planned or available
2. Any alternative approach to retrieve invoices by patient or encounter date

Current Invoice endpoint only supports `GET /FHIR/Invoice?_id={invoiceId}`. This blocks the ability to check existing invoices before prompting a GP to bill.

Billing completeness module will proceed with appointment-driven prompting model in the interim (see clinicpro-dashboard.md). If Medtech adds patient search, the module can be improved to skip the prompt when an invoice already exists.
