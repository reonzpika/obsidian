Anna Bell (Clinical Support, The Doctors Kerikeri) replied to a Capture outreach email. She asked two questions:

1. They have a very old tablet — would the app work on it?
2. They don't have a separate phone for photos.

This is a warm inbound lead. Reply by email (Anna.Bell@kerikerimedical.co.nz).

## Reply guidance

- ClinicPro Capture runs in any modern browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge). If the tablet can run a browser, it can run Capture. Minimum: Android 8+ or iOS 13+. Very old tablets (pre-2018) may struggle — ask what model or OS version if she is unsure.
- No separate phone required. The tablet or any browser-capable device at the point of care is all that is needed. The PWA captures the image on the device and sends directly to Medtech.
- Offer a short screen-share demo if she wants to see it in action.
- Confirm they are on Medtech Evolution (required for the ALEX integration).

Keep the reply warm and practical — this is a practice-level contact, not a decision-maker. Goal is to get her to forward to the practice manager or owner GP.
