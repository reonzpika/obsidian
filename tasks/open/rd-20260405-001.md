# GP Clinical Review Outreach

Securing 2-3 practising NZ GPs to review the Inbox Helper and Care Gap Finder clinical specifications for face validity and real-world fit before proceeding to Step 2 (literature review and architecture research).

## Context

Step 1 task specs are complete and grounded in published NZ guidelines. External GP review strengthens the MBIE evidence base and surfaces any clinical blind spots before they propagate into synthetic data design and architecture evaluation.

Review ask: 20-30 minutes, focused on urgency taxonomy, boundary cases, and care gap trigger logic. Not a full document review. Not an endorsement request.
