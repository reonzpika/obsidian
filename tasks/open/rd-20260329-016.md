## Context

We're an MBIE-funded R&D programme building a NZ-sovereign clinical LLM for GP workflow. We don't yet know exactly what regulatory work needs to be done — that's what we're trying to find out. The goal is to send 2–3 full-service consultancies a brief on our project and let them tell us what compliance obligations we have and what they'd charge to help us meet them. We'll compare their responses and decide what to actually do and who to engage.
