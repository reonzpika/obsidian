# ClinicPro Capture — 6-Month NZ Marketing Design

## 1. Context and assumptions

ClinicPro Capture is a mobile web app that photographs clinical images and commits them to patient records in Medtech Evolution via the ALEX API. Status as of 2026-04-15: awaiting Medtech production sign-off (expected imminently, within weeks). This plan assumes sign-off lands on schedule and direct commercial marketing can begin.

**Scope of this plan: NZ-only direct sales.** The Lawrence Peterson / Medtech AU bundle deal is tracked separately and excluded from this plan.

### Product and pricing

- Capture integrates with Medtech Evolution only. Indici, MyPractice, BPS are not on the near-term roadmap.
- Sale is **per clinic**, not per individual GP.
- Buyer is usually the practice manager or owner GP.
- Pricing: FTE-based tiers, $10–$60/month per active facility, 30-day free trial.
- Approximate ACV: $360–$720/year per practice.

### Supporting assets

- **Referral-images** is a live standalone $5/mo tool for individual NZ GPs (no PMS integration). It is used here as a top-of-funnel lead engine for Capture, not as a product being promoted for its own sake.
- Warm list: ~60+ NZ GPs on Medtech Evolution already paying for referral-images. They are **champions**, not buyers, but they can advocate internally.
- NZ Partnership Agreement (Medtech NZ) is in final negotiation. Signing unlocks co-marketing levers in Phase 3.

### Goal

25–50 paying Medtech Evolution NZ practices by end of Q3 2026 (~6 months). TAM is ~500 practices, so target is 5–10% penetration.

## 2. Target customer segments

### Horizontal baseline
- Any Medtech Evolution GP practice in NZ (~500 total).

### Clinical image intensity (highest urgency first)
- Skin cancer / mole-check clinics (high volume, compliance-driven).
- Wound care services, including nurse-led wound clinics inside practices.
- Minor surgery / procedural GPs.
- Urgent care clinics on Medtech (RNZCUC network).
- Rural / remote practices (distance from specialists).
- Sports medicine clinics.
- Travel / sexual health clinics.

### Structural leverage
- PHO / IPA networks: ProCare (~150), Pegasus (~100), Tāmaki Health (60+), Third Age, East Health, WellSouth.
- Multi-site clinic groups: Green Cross, Local Doctors, Tend.
- Corporate / occupational health clinics (ACC photo documentation).
- New / opening practices.
- Teaching / registrar-heavy practices.

### Buyer personas
- Practice managers: operational ROI, time savings, ACC compliance.
- Owner GPs / clinical directors: clinical quality and efficiency.
- PHO clinical governance / digital leads: for anchor deals.

## 5. Phased plan

### Phase 1 — Weeks 1–4: Warm-list blitz

Goal: 5–10 paying/trial practices from the warm list. These become the reference customers that unlock every subsequent phase.

| ID | Action | Effort |
|---|---|---|
| P1.1 | Ship /about-capture landing page: pricing, demo video, "book a call" CTA, trial signup | 1–2 days |
| P1.2 | In-app banner in referral-images: "Your practice on Medtech? Skip screenshotting — Capture posts straight to patient notes. Book a 10-min demo." | 1 day dev |
| P1.3 | Personal email to ~60 referral-images NZ GP champions. Offer: 3 months free Capture for their practice if they introduce the practice manager | 1 day write and send |
| P1.4 | Refer-a-practice bounty page: $200 credit or 3 months free per introduced practice | 0.5 day |
| P1.5 | 3-minute demo Loom + case-study template | 1 day |

**Success gate:** 5+ paying or trial practices signed by end of week 4. If below 3 by week 4, pause outbound, diagnose (product gaps, price objections, wrong champion segment), fix before proceeding.

### Phase 2 — Weeks 3–8: Outbound engine + MSP intros

Goal: add 5–10 practices. Outbound requires 2–3 live references from Phase 1.

| ID | Action | Effort |
|---|---|---|
| P2.1 | Named-account spreadsheet: all ~500 Medtech Evolution NZ practices, researched practice-manager names and emails | 3–5 days (may outsource research for $300–500) |
| P2.2 | Cold email sequence (3-touch over 2 weeks) + LinkedIn connect. Segmented messaging by practice type: skin/derm, urgent care, rural, horizontal | ~5h/wk ongoing |
| P2.3 | MSP coffee meetings with GCT, GTB, Pure Logic. Pitch referral fee / year-one rev share with white-glove onboarding. **Schedule weeks 5–8, after ≥2 live reference practices are signed from Phase 1.** | 1 meeting each |
| P2.4 | Founder LinkedIn cadence: 2 posts/wk as GP-founder. Clinical vignettes, before/after screenshots | 30min per post |

### Phase 3 — Months 2–4: PHO probes

Goal: fire opportunistic shots. PHO outcomes are binary and not required to hit target.

| ID | Action | Effort |
|---|---|---|
| P3.1 | PHO outreach: pitch ProCare, Pegasus, Tāmaki Health, East Health | 2h per PHO |
| P3.2 | ACC photo-documentation angle: blog post + targeted outbound to occupational health clinics | 1 day + ongoing outreach |

(Medtech NZ co-marketing is absorbed into the partnership agreement workstream once signed; not run as a standalone step here.)

| ID | Action | Effort |

| P4.3 | Free CME webinar on clinical image documentation in general practice | ~15h prep + 1h delivery |

## 6. KPIs and review cadence

Weekly review, tracked on a simple spreadsheet:

- **Qualified demos booked** (leading).
- **Trial starts** (mid).
- **Paid conversions** (lagging).
- **Cost per acquisition** (gate for paid / trade media spend).
- **Active referring champions** (gate for Phase 2 MSP outreach).

## 7. Stop / adjust triggers

- Champion email (P1.3) produces <10 replies → product-message fit problem. Fix before outbound.
- Cold outbound (P2.2) converts <0.5% after 100 sends → rewrite or pivot to niche messaging.
- MSP coffee (P2.3) all decline → drop Channel 8 for this cycle, revisit after 15 live practices.
- Phase 1 gate miss (<3 paying by week 4) → pause Phase 2, diagnose.

## 8. Known unknowns

- Exact composition of referral-images base (assumed ~60+ NZ Medtech GPs; needs data pull to confirm).
- Medtech NZ partnership agreement signing date (affects Phase 3 newsletter dependency).
- PHO procurement cycles and governance lead times.
- Practice-count totals for tier-1 MSPs (GTB, Pure Logic, Netcare) — not disclosed publicly.
- Whether ACC formally requires photo documentation for specific injury categories (would strengthen occupational-health messaging; worth checking).

## 9. Explicit non-goals

- No paid ad spend this cycle.
- No conference booths this cycle.
- No promotion of referral-images as a product (it is only a lead-gen asset).
- No AU bundle work (separate plan with Lawrence Peterson).
- No PMS expansion beyond Medtech Evolution.
