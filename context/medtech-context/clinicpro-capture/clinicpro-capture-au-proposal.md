# ClinicPro Capture: AU Bundle Indicative Terms

**From**: Dr Ryo Eguchi, Founder, ClinicPro (NexWave Solutions Ltd, NZ)
**To**: Lawrence Peterson, GM Integration & Infrastructure, Medtech Global
**Date**: 20 April 2026
**Reference**: Response to your 12 April enquiry on licence count and per-licence cost

---

## Summary

Indicative commercial terms for bundling ClinicPro Capture into Medtech Evolution AU as a practice value-add. Per-active-practice pricing with annual minimum commitment. 3-year initial term. Launch target May-June 2026, aligned with Medtech Evolution AU G2M.

## Scope

Image capture and ALEX `/DocumentReference` POST only. Additional modules are out of scope for this agreement and require separate commercial terms.

## Pricing

**AUD 30 per active practice per month.**

*Active practice* = one with at least one registered user AND at least one successful capture commit in the billing month.

Per-practice fee is inclusive of cloud infrastructure running costs on the ClinicPro side.

## Annual minimum commitment

Medtech commits to a minimum of **50 active practices per year**, equivalent to **AUD 18,000 per year**, for each of the 3 years of the initial term.

Where onboarded active practices exceed 50 in any year, the full per-active-practice fee applies to all practices above the minimum. Where onboarded practices fall short, the minimum payment still applies.

## Term

3-year initial term from contract execution. Renewal and termination mechanics to be detailed in the contract.

## Launch window

AU regionalisation engineering commences on contract execution. Target go-live May-June 2026 to align with Medtech Evolution AU G2M.

## Data residency

ClinicPro Capture is hosted in Sydney (AWS ap-southeast-2). Meets Australian data residency requirements.

## What's next

Remaining commercial items, including payment cadence, tax structure, support model, training, branding approach, compliance artefacts, and contract detail, to be worked through following our 22 April meeting.
