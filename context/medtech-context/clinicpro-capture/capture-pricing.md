# ClinicPro Capture — pricing

> Authoritative pricing. Any plan, email, or landing page copy that contradicts this is stale.

**Tagline:** Clinical photo capture for Medtech Evolution, done properly.

## Pricing model

Annual subscription per practice. Renews based on enrolled patient count at renewal date. All prices exclude GST.

| Enrolled patients | Annual fee |
|---|---|
| Under 2,000 | NZ$299 + GST |
| 2,000 to 4,000 | NZ$799 + GST |
| 4,001 to 7,000 | NZ$1,500 + GST |
| 7,000+ | Contact for pricing |

## What is included

- **One subscription, whole practice** — all GPs, nurses, admin. No per-user charges.
- **No setup fee** — no per-image charges, no hidden costs.
- **Guided onboarding** — help getting set up and connected to Medtech via ALEX.
- **Works on any Medtech Evolution** — on-premise, private cloud, or Medtech Cloud.
- **Inbox Scan delivery** — images land in the only location accessible to all referral systems (ALEX SR, ERMS, HealthLink).
- **Automatic format conversion** — converted to TIFF, universally accepted by referral systems.
- **Automatic resizing** — compressed to meet Medtech size requirements. No manual steps, no failed uploads.
- **Photos never saved to your phone** — images go direct to Medtech, not the camera roll.
- **HIPC-compliant** — no patient data stored server-side, EXIF including GPS stripped automatically.
- **Full audit trail** — every commit logged with practitioner ID, facility ID, and timestamp.
- **Ongoing support and updates** — all future updates for the duration of the subscription.

## Frequently asked questions

**Q: How do I know which tier I am on?**
A: Base your tier on your current PHO enrolled patient count — the number on your PHO dashboard.

**Q: What if my enrolled patient number changes during the year?**
A: Your subscription is fixed for 12 months. Tier updates at renewal based on enrolled count at that time.

**Q: What happens if I do not renew?**
A: Access is suspended at end of subscription period. Reminder 7 days before expiry.

**Q: We are a corporate network or have multiple sites.**
A: Contact ryo@clinicpro.co.nz for per-site or network pricing.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes. Contact ryo@clinicpro.co.nz to arrange a trial for your practice.
